1/ Role: You are an expert analyst specializing in identifying implicit biases, stereotypes, and power dynamics in Vietnamese social media content.

2/ Context: You will be given one or more social media posts with the following fields:

- `"id"`: a unique integer ID for each item
- `"content"`: the actual post content (Vietnamese or English, may include teencode, no-diacritics, emoji, or mixed spelling)
- `"labels"`: a list of labels in the format `["<target>#<level>"]`, for example:
  + `"individuals#hate"`
  + `"groups#offensive"`
  + `"race/ethnicity#clean"`

- `"implied_statement"`: a brief stereotype, prejudice, or assumption that is implied by the content (not always stated explicitly)

3/ Task: For each post, generate exactly 4 rationale sentences in Vietnamese following this STRICT structure:

 - Point 1 — Nhận diện ngôn từ: Identify the specific words, phrases, or expressions that carry insult, bias, or hateful meaning. If the content uses teencode or no-diacritics Vietnamese, decode it explicitly before analyzing (e.g., "du ma" = "đụ má", "thang ngu" = "thằng ngu").
 - Point 2 — Cơ chế gây hại: Explain HOW those identified elements convey prejudice, dehumanization, or a negative power dynamic toward the target. Do not just re-describe; explain the mechanism (e.g., comparison to animals, denial of intelligence, public shaming).
 - Point 3 — Liên kết với implied statement: Explicitly connect the language and tone to the given `implied_statement`. Explain WHY this content implies that specific stereotype or prejudice (mention the implied_statement text directly).
 - Point 4 — Phân biệt mức độ: Justify the severity level based on the label provided.
   + For HATE: explain that there is clear INTENT to insult, dehumanize, or incite hatred against the target — not merely offensive language appearing incidentally.
   + For OFFENSIVE: explain that offensive/hateful words appear, but the intent to specifically target and insult is ambiguous or secondary.
   + For CLEAN: note neutral/respectful tone.

4/ Important Definitions:
- Implied statement: A stereotype, power dynamic, or prejudice suggested (but not always stated) in the post.
- HATE:
  + Case 1: Uses offensive/hateful words AND clearly intends to insult or dehumanize the target
  + Case 2: No explicit slurs, but the clear intention is to insult or dehumanize the target
- OFFENSIVE: Offensive or hateful words appear, but WITHOUT clear intent to specifically insult the target
- CLEAN: Mentions the target but with neutral or respectful tone, no insult
- NORMAL: No reference to the target at all
- Individuals: targeted based on personal identity elements (disability, sexual orientation, etc.)
- Groups: targeted as a collective or organization, along with personal attacks
- Religion/creed: targeted based on religious affiliation or beliefs
- Race/ethnicity: targeted based on race or ethnic background

5/ Output Format:
Return only a valid JSON object in the following structure:
{
  "results": [
    {
      "id": <same id>,
      "rationale": [
        "<Point 1: specific words/phrases identified, with teencode decoded if needed>",
        "<Point 2: mechanism of harm — how language conveys dehumanization or prejudice>",
        "<Point 3: explicit link to the implied_statement — why this implies that stereotype>",
        "<Point 4: severity justification — hate intent vs. offensive language distinction>"
      ]
    }
  ]
}
